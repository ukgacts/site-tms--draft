Pulseframes is all about storytelling. And today, the most popular way to tell stories is through the “story” format—used on platforms like Instagram, Facebook, and Telegram. So what makes Pulseframes different—and why should it matter to you?

01

Ownership & control

Unlike traditional social media stories that disappear after 24 hours, a Pulseframe story can live forever. You fully own it, and it stays available as long as you want.

On platforms like Instagram or Facebook: Your content lives on their platform It can be removed, limited, or buried by algorithms With Pulseframes: You control the content No dependency on platform rules or algorithm changes

02

Unlimited linking possibilities

With Pulseframes, you can include as many links as you like. There are no restrictions. On other platforms, you’re often limited—for example, needing a large following just to unlock basic linking features. Pulseframes removes those barriers completely.

03

Share anywhere, effortlessly

Your Pulseframe story can be shared across any platform using a simple link—making it easy to distribute your content wherever your audience is.
