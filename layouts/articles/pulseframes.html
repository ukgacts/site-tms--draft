PulseFrames™

Stories,
Rebuilt for the Open Web.

A mobile-first storytelling format that lives beyond platforms— discoverable, searchable, and fully yours.

PulseFrames function like microsites—or as some clients describe them, satellites for your brand. Instead of being locked inside apps, they exist on the open web, where they can be indexed, shared, and continuously drive traffic to your ecosystem.
