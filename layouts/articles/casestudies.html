Case Study

Pori Jazz Festival

Designing the Sound of the Brand

Pori Jazz is one of Europe's most respected music festivals, hosting world-class artists and attracting audiences from around the globe.

THE CHALLENGE

Potential attendees often need more than schedules and ticket links — they need to feel the atmosphere and clearly understand what the experience will be like.

THE APPROACH

By combining storytelling with practical resources, the Pulseframe creates a bridge between inspiration and action. Instead of simply reading about the festival, visitors can explore the experience visually, discover the destination, and easily access the information they need to plan their visit. The result is a clearer, more engaging introduction to Pori Jazz, designed to inspire international audiences and guide them smoothly from curiosity to attendance.

Link Hub

Some links are included through attachments, which allow users to swipe up or tap to access additional information without interrupting the flow of the story. This method works well for resources that require more detailed exploration, such as accommodation listings or full ticket pages. Other links are placed directly on the slides as clickable elements. These links are visible while the user is viewing the slide and allow for quick access to specific pages, such as a restaurant, attraction, or booking option mentioned in the story.

Story Architecture

Our goal with Pulseframe was to transform practical information into an engaging story that helps visitors imagine their visit before they arrive. The story combines visual storytelling with practical guidance, making the journey from discovery to ticket purchase seamless.

Within the Pulseframe, visitors can explore: Short video moments that capture the atmosphere and energy of the festival Direct links to relevant resources such as accommodation and ticket pages Helpful travel information for international guests Highlights of the city of Pori, including attractions, food, and local experiences
