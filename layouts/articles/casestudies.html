About

GlowSkin, a fast-growing organic skincare brand, was struggling with low online conversions despite strong social media engagement. They approached our team to redesign their website and optimize their customer journey.

The Challenge Although GlowSkin had: 50K+ Instagram followers Consistent traffic (15,000 monthly visitors) High product ratings They faced several problems: 1.2% conversion rate (below industry average) High cart abandonment (78%) Slow website speed (6+ seconds load time) Confusing navigation and checkout process The brand needed a seamless, mobile-friendly experience that reflected its premium identity.
