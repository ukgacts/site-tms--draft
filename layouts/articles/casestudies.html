Case Study

Pori Jazz Festival

Designing the Sound of the Brand

Pori Jazz is one of Europe's most respected music festivals, hosting world-class artists and attracting audiences from around the globe.

THE CHALLENGE

Potential attendees often need more than schedules and ticket links — they need to feel the atmosphere and clearly understand what the experience will be like.

THE APPROACH

By combining storytelling with practical resources, the Pulseframe creates a bridge between inspiration and action. Instead of simply reading about the festival, visitors can explore the experience visually, discover the destination, and easily access the information they need to plan their visit. The result is a clearer, more engaging introduction to Pori Jazz, designed to inspire international audiences and guide them smoothly from curiosity to attendance.

07 — Story System

Story Architecture

Story Architecture

Slide 1 (Cover) "Festival Highlights" We collected album covers from the artists performing at the festival to give a visual preview of the lineup. Slide 2 "Explore the Festival Venue" We offered a guide to exploring the festival location, highlighting must-see areas and unique spots on site. Slide 3 "Accommodation Options" We suggested several accommodation choices for visitors. Understanding that festival-goers often enjoy alternative stays, we focused on budget-friendly and comfortable options. Slide 4 "Getting to Pori" Traveling from Helsinki to Pori is a long journey. We provided clear directions and tips on how to reach Pori easily. Slide 5 "Discover Pori" Did you know Pori is a beautiful spa destination? Many international visitors are unaware, so we highlighted other attractions and activities to enjoy while there. Slide 6 "Taste the Local Cuisine" Finnish cuisine is delicious! We recommended restaurants and eateries where visitors can enjoy authentic local dishes. Slide 7 "English-Friendly for International Visitors" To ensure a welcoming experience for international guests, we highlighted locations and services where English is spoken. Slide 8 "Get Your Tickets" Direct link to purchase festival tickets online for easy access.

Designing the Story Experience

Pulseframe transforms practical information into a visual journey that helps visitors imagine their festival experience before arriving.

The story combines short video moments, travel guidance and local highlights to guide users from discovery to ticket purchase.
