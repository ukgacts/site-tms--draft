Case Study

Pori Jazz Festival

Designing the Sound of the Brand

Pori Jazz is one of Europe's most respected music festivals, hosting world-class artists and attracting audiences from around the globe.

The Challenge

Potential attendees often need more than schedules and ticket links — they need to feel the atmosphere and clearly understand what the experience will be like.

The Approach

By combining storytelling with practical resources, the Pulseframe creates a bridge between inspiration and action. Instead of simply reading about the festival, visitors can explore the experience visually, discover the destination, and easily access the information they need to plan their visit.

The result is a clearer, more engaging introduction to Pori Jazz, designed to inspire international audiences and guide them smoothly from curiosity to attendance.
