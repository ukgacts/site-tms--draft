Format

Stories

Stories are brief, engaging, and interactive. They create a natural user experience, allowing information to flow seamlessly instead of being presented as static facts.

People connect with stories. They remember them. They share them. When content is too long, it becomes easy to ignore — stories solve that problem.

Unlike social media stories, Pulseframe stories are permanent. They live on the open web and continuously drive traffic.

Format

Short Videos

Short-form video is one of the most effective ways to communicate today.

According to research, 64% of marketers use short-form video to stay competitive — and for good reason. Audiences prefer visual content that delivers value quickly without demanding too much time.

We create short videos that are not only visually engaging but also carefully crafted — starting and ending in sync with the rhythm of the music to maximize emotional impact.

Format

Quote Cards

Quote cards highlight powerful insights from interviews, testimonials, or key brand messages. They present short, meaningful statements in a format that is easy to scan and visually appealing.

On desktop, they work especially well as horizontal carousels or structured layouts, while on mobile they adapt to more dynamic formats.

Beyond aesthetics, quote cards also support SEO and content strategy, helping attract new audiences and reinforce brand authority.

Format

Reels

Reels are dynamic collections of images and video clips combined into a single, engaging format.

They are perfect for sharing moments, building excitement, and creating a stronger emotional connection with your audience — whether promoting an upcoming event or highlighting past experiences.
