Formats We Use to Reach Your Audience

Stories

Stories are brief, engaging, and interactive. They create a natural user experience, allowing information to flow seamlessly instead of being presented as static facts.

People connect with stories. They remember them. They share them. When content is too long, it becomes easy to ignore — stories solve that problem.

Unlike social media stories, Pulseframe stories are permanent. They live on the open web and continuously drive traffic to your website or any platform you choose.

Short Videos

Short-form video is one of the most effective ways to communicate today. Audiences prefer visual content that delivers value quickly without taking too much time.

We create videos that are not only visually engaging but also carefully crafted — starting and ending in sync with the rhythm of the music to maximize emotional impact.

Quote Cards

Quote cards highlight powerful insights from interviews, testimonials, or key brand messages in a concise and visually appealing format.

They work especially well as horizontal carousels on desktop and adapt naturally to dynamic mobile layouts.

Beyond aesthetics, they can support SEO and content strategy, helping attract new audiences and reinforce brand authority.

Reels

Reels combine images and video into a single dynamic format.

They are perfect for sharing moments, building excitement, and creating a stronger emotional connection with your audience — whether promoting an upcoming event or showcasing past experiences.
